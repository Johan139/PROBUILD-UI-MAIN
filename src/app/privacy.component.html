This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Platform, including licensed professionals, construction firms, developers, students, and general users, regardless of location. If you are located in California or another jurisdiction with specific privacy rights, additional sections below may apply.

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain access to the Platform

  • Improve, troubleshoot, and analyze the Platform’s performance
  • Respond to customer support inquiries
